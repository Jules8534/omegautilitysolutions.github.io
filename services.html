Services

Underground Construction

Directional Boring

For those challenging areas to locate and place piping or cables, we utilize our directional boring machines to get the tough jobs done. we host a team of construction experts to operate the machinery with precision and inset necessary materials like conduits, casing, and sewer lines into their repsective environments.

Trenching

Our trenching service provides optimal, inground construction with an experienced and safety-minded team. We install telephone lines and mains for gas and water supply throughout our local towns and cities. We clear digging sites for preparation, construct internal trenches, implement support braces for excavations sites, provide scaffolding materials, and eliminate work locations of construction elements and debris after completed projects.

Plowing

For building construction, cleanup, and snow removal(if needed), Omega Utility Solutions, LLC utilizes a collection of plows. We plow construction sites for material and debris removal and remove snow when necessary during the construction process. Our plows are operated by a team of highly skilled professionals with excellent driving and mechanic skills.

Fiber Services

Installation of Fiber & Fiber Pull

Our fiber optic services serve a range of purposes. We install telephone communications, LAN lines, and lighting for industrial buildings. With our fiber installation work, we help businesses and organizations operate to their highest potential. We value their commitments to their clientele and prioritize our own with a devotion to a quality job and customer service. At Omega Utility, our fiber pulling mechanisms enable us to provide our customers premium signal strength and reliable networks to ensure fiber optics that yield productive results. All fiber pulling devices we use are securely designed to stabilize every load and complete a sufficient transfer every time.
