Services

Underground Construction

Directional Boring

For those challenging areas to locate and place piping or cables, we utilize our directional boring machines to get the tough jobs done. we host a team of construction experts to operate the machinery with precision and inset necessary materials like conduits, casing, and sewer lines into their repsective environments.

Trenching

Our trenching service provides optimal, inground construction with an experienced and safety-minded team. We install telephone lines and mains for gas and water supply throughout our local towns and cities. We clear digging sites for preparation, construct internal trenches, implement support braces for excavations sites, provide scaffolding materials, and eliminate work locations of construction elements and debris after completed projects.

Plowing

For building construction, cleanup, and snow removal(if needed), Omega Utility Solutions, LLC utilizes a collection of plows. We plow construction sites for material and debris removal and remove snow when necessary during the construction process. Our plows are operated by a team of highly skilled professionals with excellent driving and mechanic skills.

Large Diameter Bores

To drill and access oil and gas lines, large diameter bores are used. Our boring service is vital for supplying essential resources to our communities and we do our best to deliver fast and efficient service when our clients are in need. Our cutting techniques are precise and pristine, ensuring optimal entry.

Installation of Manholes

Omega Utility Solutions, LLC boasts a team of trained construction experts who prioritize safety and prime results. With steady hands and keen vision for placement, we make manhole installations, effortless. We install manholes in a variety of surfaces, creating entry points for utility system inspections, sanitization, and maintenance.

Specializing in Solid Formation

Offering construction services for residential, industrial, and commercial clients is where we thrive. We specialize in forming solid formations for driveways, parking lots, and entry ways for various properties. With expert accuracy and precision, our construction team maneuvers our machines with class, laying solid formations for multiple surfaces. For us, consistency is key.

Other Services

Telecommunication Services

Communication is vital in this digital age and Omega Utility Solutions, LLC is determined to connect, repair, and replace telecom lines for clients in need of communication connections. We know business is impossible without collaboration through communicative means, so we value the need for our telecommunication services and serve our community will excellence.

Gas Services

We provide professional gas line installations and repairs to ensure all heating and electrical operations are running efficiently. Gas is a cost-effective and reliable way to heat and light up a home or office building and we do our best to supply direct gas connections to those who need them.

Water Services

Water is an essential to life and Omega Utility Solutions, LLC works to provide clients a water supply by offering installation and repair services for water lines. Our services are not limited to residential properties and can serve industrial or commercial buildings. Water line demands can be met with our certified team and construction expertise.

Fiber Services

With our fiber installation work, we help businesses and organizations operate to their highest potential. We value their commitments to their clientele and prioritize our own with a devotion to a quality job and customer service. At Omega Utility, our fiber pulling mechanisms enable us to provide our customers premium signal strength and reliable networks to ensure fiber optics that yield productive results. All fiber pulling devices we use are securely designed to stabilize every load and complete a sufficient transfer every time.
